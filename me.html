About Me

Yiheng Chi (Undergrad, Year 4) is currently a senior in Computer Engineering at Purdue University. He has been working with Prof. Stanley H. Chan since December 2016. He is working on very fast image denoising algorithms for computational photography applications. His current research interests include image and video restoration, imaging on mobile devices, denoising, computer vision, machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Chi also has work experiences of Engineering Intern at General Electric, Shanghai, China from June 2016 to July 2016
